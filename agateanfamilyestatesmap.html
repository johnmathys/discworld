Skills Required for Missions

 ^ Contents ^

Getting past the guards:

  • Using Sneak: co.st.in x, co.st.ou x, co.hi.pe x
  • Using Fear: fa.ri.of.ta 220 bonus
  • Using Stasis: fa.ri.of.ta
  • Using Endless Halls
  • Using the spell Sorsalsean's Seismic Eruption (SSE)

Sneaky Entrances:

  • Tang Estate cliff. ad.mo.sw plus adventuring.movement.climbing.rock 220+ bonus * * * DO NOT CLIMB WHILE TIRED * * *
  • McSweeney Estate Men's Quarters: adventuring.movement.climbing.tree ? bonus
  • McSweeney Estate Women's Quarters: adventuring.movement.climbing.rock 220+ bonus
  • Hong Estate Tree

Haiku:

For the Haiku form: people.culture.agatean 145+ bonus, For the elegance required: crafts.arts.calligraphy 185+ bonus

Alter Information:

80 levels of written Agatean, adventuring.direction 195+ bonus

Capture Pet:

crafts.hunting.trapping 160+ bonus

Investigate:

perception 200+ bonus

Milk Snake:

rummage: cr.hu.tracking 180+ bonus - this also seems to cover the divert, seize and milk skills required.
